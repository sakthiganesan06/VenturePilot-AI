SaaS Startup Key Metrics: MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue), Churn Rate (industry benchmark 5-10% monthly for SMB SaaS), CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), LTV (Lifetime Value), LTV:CAC ratio (target >3:1), NRR (Net Revenue Retention, target >100%), Payback Period (target <12 months). A healthy SaaS startup should achieve ₹1Cr ARR within 18-24 months of launch.
